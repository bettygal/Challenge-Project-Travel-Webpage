A Tour To Africa's Most Beautiful Campus

History of Obafemi Awolowo University

Obafemi Awolowo University was Founded in 1961 as University of Ife, the University was renamed in 1987 as Obafemi Awolowo University to honor chief Obafemi Awolowo.
It was Located in Ile-Ife, Osun State, an ancient Yoruba city. It was located at entrance of Ile-Ife. OAU comprises of international and local students.

The university is Popularly known as the African most beautiful Campus due to some buildings which are characterized by a modern slanting styles

Unique Structures in OAU

Israeli architect and Bauhaus alumna Arieh Sharon, in collaboration with Nigerian office A.A. Egbor and AMY Company, continuously worked on the school’s structures and master plan for a span of 25 years,
completing what is known as Africa’s "most beautiful campus".

Many OAU structures will leave you wondering how on earth the contractors were able to come with a design like this and not just that, how were they able to erect such masterpiece. When you take a walk around OAU campus, you will be surprised at the number of beautiful and mind-blowing structures you will come across.
